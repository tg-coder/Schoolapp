Campus Life

School Library

The library is extremley big.

The library is extremely big. It has hundreds and hundreds of books you can chose from, from Thea Stilton to Pride and Prejudice. Everyone has their own library card and they can go to the librarian and borrow a book. There are different sections to chose from. And everything is labeled for it to be easier. To check if a book is there in the library of if it is currently borrowed by someone else, you can either ask the librarian or use a computer to search it. Reading is certainly encouraged in the school by teachers.

playground

Safe playground equipment

The playgrounds equipment is very very safe and are lots of fun to play on. It has things like rock climbing, slides, climbing frame, climbable slanted poles,etc. Children love it and prefer to run around an play pretend games on the equipment. It is very spacious and has greenery around.
